    Who is at risk?

    People with heart and lung conditions are most affected by air pollution.

    To find out if you are at risk, consult the health guide, or your physician.

    Visit the national AQHI Web site to learn more about the AQHI.

    Did you know...?

    Negative health effects increase as air pollution worsens.


    The AQHI is an initiative between Environment Canada, Health Canada, Environment and Conservation, Newfoundland and Labrador and Health and Community Services, Newfoundland and Labrador.
